Welcome

Earth & Essence Foundation is dedicated to the integrated growth of people and the planet through sustainable, holistic practices. We blend traditional knowledge with modern solutions across multiple service sectors.

About Us

We are a not-for-profit initiative supporting communities with services that promote health, sustainability, and self-reliance. Our goal is to nurture body, mind, and environment through wellness, organic living, and responsible service delivery.

Our Services

  • Wellness: Ayush, Siddha, Yoga, and Allopathy Integration
  • Hospitality: Eco-stay experiences and traditional care
  • Agro: Organic farming, palm cultivation, green produce
  • Animal Husbandry: Sustainable livestock and care
  • Laundry & Security: Community-based employment services
  • Food: Healthy meals through cloud kitchens and local hubs
